Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

Rhizome is a self-hosted note-taking application. This policy explains what the Rhizome apps (web and iOS) do with your data. It is written for the instance hosted at this address; if you run your own server, you are the data controller for the notes it stores.

Who runs this server

This instance is operated by its owner (the person who set up the server). The Rhizome software itself is not a hosted service run by a third party — your notes live only on the server you connect to.

What data is stored

  • Your account: username and a hashed password.
  • Your notes and their history: the text, structure, and revision history of the outlines you create.
  • Uploaded files: images and attachments you add to notes. These are stored encrypted at rest on the server.
  • Location data (optional): if you attach a location to a note, the coordinates are stored on the server and resolved to an address using the OpenStreetMap Nominatim service.
  • Device name (optional): a name you set for your device, shown in page history so you can tell which device made a change.

Where your data goes

Your notes, files, and settings are sent only to the Rhizome server you sign in to. They are not shared with the app's developer or any advertising or analytics company. The apps contain no third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.

The only external service contacted on your behalf is OpenStreetMap (for reverse geocoding and map tiles), and only when you use a location feature. Coordinates you look up are sent to OpenStreetMap for that purpose.

Camera and photo access (iOS)

The iOS app requests camera and photo-library access only when you choose to attach an image to a note. Photos you select are uploaded to your Rhizome server and nowhere else.

Device storage

The iOS app stores your app settings (such as font size and server URL) locally using the system's standard preferences store, and keeps a session cookie so you stay signed in. Optionally, it can lock the app behind Face ID / Touch ID.

Data retention and deletion

Your notes remain on the server until you delete them. To delete your account and all associated data, contact the server's operator. There is no third party holding copies of your data.

Children

Rhizome is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Contact

For questions about your data or to request deletion, contact the operator of this server.
